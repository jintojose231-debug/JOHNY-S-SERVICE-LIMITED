Commercial Deep Cleaning Services

JSL clean offers expert deep cleaning services customized to suit the specific requirements of your commercial space. Maintaining a clean work environment plays a key role in making a strong impression on clients while also supporting the health and safety of your team. Whether you operate an office, retail outlet, or hospitality business, our cleaning solutions are designed to serve a broad range of industries and professional settings.

What Our Service Includes

General Cleaning Services

  • We thoroughly mop and scrub all flooring surfaces.
  • Light switches, door handles, and fixtures are carefully wiped down.
  • Ledges, vents, and baseboards are dusted to eliminate buildup.
  • High-traffic areas like restrooms and kitchens receive detailed attention to ensure full sanitation.

Furniture and Cabinet Cleaning

  • All furniture is moved, cleaned, and returned to its place.
  • Cabinets, drawers, and shelving units are emptied and wiped clean inside and out.

Commercial Carpet Cleaning

  • Our carpet cleaning process includes deodorizing, spot treatment, and protective care.
  • We use low-moisture encapsulation methods for quicker drying and minimal disruption.

Tile and Grout Cleaning

  • We remove stubborn dirt from tile and grout with gentle yet highly effective cleaning techniques.
  • Surfaces are restored and sealed to preserve their condition and appearance.

Upholstery Cleaning

  • Office furniture is deep-cleaned to remove dust, allergens, and pollutants.
  • Our process helps extend the life of your upholstery while creating a cleaner, healthier workspace.

Daily, Weekly, and Monthly Cleaning Services

Daily Cleaning Procedures

To maintain a consistently clean environment with prompt attention:

  • Debris Removal: Conduct a visual check for any debris or paper and dispose of them properly.
  • Vacuuming: Quickly vacuum dirt and debris, focusing on busy areas and office workspaces.
  • Spot Cleaning: Treat any stains or spots on carpets, floors, walls, furniture, and baseboards.
  • Trash Management: Empty all trash bins and replace liners as needed.
  • Surface Cleaning: Wipe down desks, counters, and frequently touched surfaces to keep them fresh and clean.

Weekly Cleaning Procedures

For more thorough and detailed upkeep:

  • Dusting: Remove dust from desks, shelves, filing cabinets, and cubicle walls.
  • Trash and Surroundings: Empty bins, replace liners, and clean the surrounding area when necessary.
  • Disinfecting: Use disinfectant wipes on telephones, calculators, keyboards, and desks to maintain hygiene.
  • Floor Care: Dust mop hard surfaces and follow up with wet mopping for a deep clean.
  • Vacuuming: Pay special attention to carpets, mats, and runners to remove dirt and allergens.

Monthly Cleaning Procedures

For a deep and comprehensive clean:

  • Fixture Cleaning: Sanitize wall-mounted units such as hand dryers and paper towel dispensers.
  • Vertical Surfaces: Clean walls and other upright surfaces using an all-purpose cleaner.
  • Floor Maintenance: Perform spray buffing or burnishing on hard floors to achieve a polished look.
  • Furniture Polishing: Apply polish to desks, tables, and chairs to enhance their appearance.
  • Vent and Fan Cleaning: Dust or vacuum vents, ceiling fans, and hard-to-reach spots.
  • Window Cleaning: Clean windows thoroughly with glass cleaner to remove smudges and fingerprints.
