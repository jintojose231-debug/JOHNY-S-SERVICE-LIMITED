Commercial Deep Cleaning

JSL clean offers expert deep cleaning services customized to suit the specific requirements of your commercial space. Maintaining a clean work environment plays a key role in making a strong impression on clients while also supporting the health and safety of your team.

What Our Service Includes

General Cleaning Services

  • We thoroughly mop and scrub all flooring surfaces.
  • Light switches, door handles, and fixtures are carefully wiped down.
  • Ledges, vents, and baseboards are dusted to eliminate buildup.
  • High-traffic areas like restrooms and kitchens receive detailed attention.

Furniture and Cabinet Cleaning

  • All furniture is moved, cleaned, and returned to its place.
  • Cabinets, drawers, and shelving units are emptied and wiped clean.

Move-In & Move-Out Cleaning

What's Included in Our Service

Kitchen Cleaning

  • Detailed cleaning of all countertops, cabinets and drawers
  • Sinks, faucets, and backsplashes are scrubbed and disinfected
  • Appliances such as the stove, oven, microwave cleaned inside and out

Bathroom Cleaning

  • Toilets, sinks, showers, and bathtubs are deeply sanitized
  • Mirrors, exhaust fans, and lighting fixtures are cleaned
  • Tile surfaces and grout lines are scrubbed

Airbnb Cleaning

We specialize in professional cleaning solutions tailored specifically for Airbnb hosts. A clean, inviting space is essential for earning great reviews and encouraging repeat guests.

Why Cleanliness Matters for Airbnb Hosts

Maintaining Standards

Cleanliness is one of Airbnb's most critical quality factors.

Enhancing Guest Satisfaction

Guests are more likely to leave positive feedback when they arrive to find a tidy space.

Mold Removal & Cleaning

Mold growth poses risks to both your health and property. Our expert mold cleaning services thoroughly eliminate mold, inhibit future growth, and help restore a safe, healthy living or working space.

Regular Maintenance Cleaning

Daily Cleaning Procedures

  • Debris removal and visual checks
  • Vacuuming busy areas and workspaces
  • Spot cleaning of stains
  • Emptying trash bins and replacing liners
  • Wiping down desks and frequently touched surfaces

Weekly Cleaning Procedures

  • Dusting desks, shelves, and cubicle walls
  • Emptying bins and cleaning surrounding areas
  • Disinfecting telephones, keyboards, and desks
  • Dust and wet mopping hard surfaces

Monthly Cleaning Procedures

  • Sanitizing wall-mounted units
  • Cleaning walls and vertical surfaces
  • Spray buffing or burnishing hard floors
  • Polishing desks, tables, and chairs
