About Us

At JOHNY'S SERVICE LIMITED, we take pride in delivering professional and reliable cleaning services across Wellington, New Zealand. With a strong commitment to quality and attention to detail, we help our clients maintain clean, healthy, and welcoming spaces.

Our team specializes in carpet cleaning, move-in and move-out deep cleaning, commercial deep cleaning, Airbnb cleaning, and mold removal. Whether you are preparing a property for new tenants, ensuring your Airbnb guests have a spotless stay, revitalizing your carpets, or tackling mold issues, we provide tailored solutions that make a real difference.

We use safe and effective methods to achieve outstanding results, giving you peace of mind and a fresh environment to enjoy. At JOHNY'S SERVICE LIMITED, our mission is simple — to make cleaning stress-free while creating spaces that shine.
